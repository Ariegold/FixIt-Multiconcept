Mythros Solution Services · Change Management Framework
Fixit Multiconcepts — Digital Transformation Impact Plan
The Mythros Change Framework assesses transformation across 5 dimensions: People, Process, Systems, Behaviours, Culture. Each dimension is scored 0–3 (0=no change, 3=high impact) and mapped from AS-IS to TO-BE. This is the standard framework applied to all Mythros client engagements.

Mythros Change Impact Matrix — Fixit Multiconcepts

Score: 0=Low impact · 3=High impact
DimensionScoreAS-IS (current state)TO-BE (with Fixit CRM)
PEOPLE2Personal Gmail, WhatsApp for all operations. No formal record system. 9 years of institutional knowledge at risk when staff leave.Every action logged in CRM. GPS-verified attendance. Role-based access. Training records tracked. Zero data loss from staff turnover.
PROCESS2All processes manual — incident reporting verbal, invoicing in Excel, vendor management via personal contacts, no project management system.Standardised digital workflows via n8n. Incident auto-logging. Invoice auto-reminders (7/14/30 day). GPS clock-in. Vendor portal with 48h follow-up.
SYSTEMS3Personal Gmail, Google Drive (recently restructured), WhatsApp only. No integration between tools. No inbound lead generation ever.Fixit CRM + 10 n8n automation workflows. WhatsApp Business API. Google Sheets sync. Gmail integration. AI-powered 24/7 front desk (WF01).
BEHAVIOURS2Staff call or WhatsApp supervisors for everything. No digital record keeping. No dashboards. Attendance tracked informally by phone.GPS app clock-in via CRM. Digital incident forms. AI classifies enquiries automatically. Morning digest sent to GM at 7am daily.
CULTURE3Growing admin frustration. Leadership has zero real-time operational visibility. Zero inbound leads in 10 years of operation.Real-time operations dashboard. AI front desk handles enquiries 24/7. Social media content generated weekly by AI. Full leadership KPI visibility.

Mythros vs traditional framework

Traditional change frameworks (e.g. ADKAR, Kotter 8-Step) are designed for large enterprise change programmes with dedicated change management teams and 12+ month timelines.

The Mythros Solution Services Framework is designed for SME digital transformations in growth markets. It is faster, more practical, and built around the realities of businesses like Fixit — lean teams, WhatsApp-first communication, and the need for visible results within 90 days.

Three phases: Awareness → Capability → Reinforcement. Delivery by Mythros throughout.

Mythros Solution Services · Hosting Recommendation
Where to host the Fixit CRM in Nigeria
The CRM frontend is a single HTML file — no server required. It calls n8n webhooks directly from the browser. Hosting is simply putting the file online at a URL Fixit staff can access.
★ Recommendation: Netlify (free) — Deploy in 2 minutes
Netlify is the fastest way to get the Fixit CRM live. Free tier, HTTPS, global CDN including Africa. No server, no server management, no monthly cost. URL: fixit-crm.netlify.app or connect the custom domain crm.fixitmulticoncepts.com.
1
Go to netlify.com → Sign up with your email (free)
2
Click Add new site → Deploy manually
3
Drag and drop the Fixit_CRM_Final.html file onto the deploy box
4
Netlify gives you a live URL immediately — e.g. bold-lion-abc123.netlify.app
5
Rename it: Site settings → Change site name → type fixit-crm → URL becomes fixit-crm.netlify.app
6
Share the URL with Temilade and the Fixit team — they can open it on any browser, laptop or phone

For multiple Mythros clients — the architecture

Each client gets their own: Netlify site (CRM frontend) + Railway app (backend) + n8n workspace on arie08.app.n8n.cloud (automations) + Meta Business account (WhatsApp).

Mythros manages all of these. Each client only sees their CRM URL. The Mythros n8n account scales to unlimited workflows. Railway charges per app but free tier covers 3–4 small clients. Total cost per new client once Fixit is built: approximately ₦0 to ₦5,000/month depending on database size.
